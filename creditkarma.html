Credit Karma (today’s MVP) – Founder Kit

Credit portal: account linking, score insights, product recommendations, alerts.

Overview

Goal: Build a personal credit portal with secure account linking, score tracking, personalized insights, and notifications.

  • Primary users: Consumers, Admin
  • Platforms: Web app, Mobile app
  • Integrations: Plaid (account linking), Experian/Equifax/TransUnion APIs
  • Revenue: Affiliate offers, partner banks

MVP Scope (must-have)

  • User signup & secure login
  • Bank/credit account linking (Plaid)
  • Credit score retrieval & display
  • Personalized credit insights & alerts

Stretch Goals (nice-to-have)

  • Credit card recommendations
  • Loan offers
  • Mobile app (React Native)
