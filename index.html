Frequently Asked Questions

How is WarpFoundary different from no-code tools?

No-code platforms (Bubble, Webflow) give you building blocks. WarpFoundary goes further: it generates a whole startup kit (MVP prototype, pitch deck, brand kit) from a simple brief. It’s less “drag-and-drop” and more “press play and review.”

What’s your moat—couldn’t anyone copy this?

Ideas can be copied, but moats come from execution. WarpFoundary builds defensibility through: (1) trademark + brand lock, (2) a growing founder community, (3) proprietary templates + training data, and (4) integrations (domains, TM checks, hosting).

Who is this for?

Solo founders, early-stage teams, accelerators, and VCs that need quick MVP validation. Also useful for agencies that want to generate branded prototypes for clients faster.

Will my ideas stay private?

Yes. Your briefs and generated projects remain private to your account. Roadmap includes optional NDA + export mode for investor sharing.

How do you make money?

Free tier for hobbyists. Pro subscription for serious founders (extra exports, TM checks, priority templates). Enterprise tier for accelerators/VCs to generate 100+ startup kits per cohort.

What about competitors like Decart or Builder.ai?

Infra players (Decart, Anthropic) focus on models. Builder.ai and others build apps, but not full founder kits (pitch, brand, TM). WarpFoundary’s wedge: “the first stop for every founder.”

What’s next on the roadmap?

Short term: polish examples + capture early-access signups. Medium term: interactive demo + founder community. Long term: partner with accelerators and expand into full-stack “startup studio as a service.”
