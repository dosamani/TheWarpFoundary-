Lovable (today’s MVP) — Founder Kit

Stockholm-based digital product design & engineering partner helping startups and enterprises ship lovable user experiences.

Overview

Goal: Partner with companies to discover, design, and launch digital products with world-class UX, robust design systems, and rapid iteration.

  • Primary clients: Startups, scale-ups, enterprises
  • Platforms: Web, iOS/Android (React Native or native), multi-platform digital products
  • Specialties: UX/UI design, product strategy, prototyping, design systems, front-end engineering
  • Revenue: Consulting engagements, fixed-scope sprints, long-term product partnerships

MVP Scope (must-have)

  • Product discovery workshop (stakeholder + user goals, risks, success metrics)
  • Clickable prototype (Figma or similar) of core flows
  • Design system starter kit (tokens, components, accessibility)
  • Implementation sprint plan (tech stack, milestones, resourcing)
  • Lightweight analytics & feedback loop plan

Stretch Goals (nice-to-have)

  • User research sessions & synthesis report
  • Usability testing on prototype with 5–8 target users
  • Marketing site hero + case-study draft
  • Foundations for multi-brand or white-label design

Core Deliverables

  • Clickable prototype of 3–5 hero screens
  • Design system starter (tokens, components, usage)
  • Architecture & implementation plan (front-end & back-end)
  • Project roadmap with risks & mitigation
  • Case-study one-pager (problem → approach → outcome)

Assumptions & Guardrails

  • Keep scope laser-focused on first live user value
  • Design system is “starter” (expand post-MVP)
  • Prototype prioritizes narrative & testability over pixel-perfect breadth
  • Accessibility AA targets from day one
