Lovable (today’s MVP) – Founder Kit

Social app for building and strengthening close personal relationships.

Overview

Goal: Create a mobile-first app that helps people deepen their connections through shared moments, messaging, and thoughtful prompts.

  • Primary users: Friends, Couples, Families
  • Platforms: iOS, Android (React Native)
  • Monetization: Subscriptions, in-app purchases

MVP Scope (must-have)

  • User signup & profiles
  • Private chat (1-to-1)
  • Daily/weekly conversation prompts
  • Photo & memory sharing

Stretch Goals (nice-to-have)

  • Couple/family calendars & reminders
  • Shared journals
  • AI-powered relationship insights
