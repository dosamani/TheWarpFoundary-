Luvable (today’s MVP) – Founder Kit

Luvable is a Swedish unicorn redefining D2C with sustainable, design-first consumer products.

Overview

Goal: Validate a direct-to-consumer brand with premium product storytelling, seamless checkout, and recurring subscriptions.

  • Primary users: Shoppers, Admin
  • Platform: Web storefront (mobile-first), lightweight admin
  • Catalog: Sustainable lifestyle products (expandable product lines)
  • Monetization: One-time sales + subscriptions

MVP Scope (must-have)

  • Home + collections + product detail pages (PDPs)
  • Cart & checkout (Stripe or Shopify Checkout)
  • Subscriptions & order history
  • User-generated reviews (text + rating)
  • CMS blocks for promos, hero banners, FAQs, policies

Stretch Goals (nice-to-have)

  • Mobile app (React Native)
  • Loyalty & referral programs
  • Localized storefronts (multi-region, multi-currency)
